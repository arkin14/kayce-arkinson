What I do

Over the last two years of college, I've learned as much inside the classroom as I have from my experience in the workforce. From trail work in Colorado to finding small business solutions back in North Carolina, I've explored an array of unique positions.

These opportunities taught me to recognize and embody leadership informed by courage, collaboration, humility, and dedication. The most impactful position I've held has been working in low-income elementary schools as a nutrition educator. Running classroom programs over 4-6 week long stints, my team and I's goal is to provide young learners with exposure to healthy eating practices. Looking ahead, I aim to pursue a service-oriented role in health promotion, building on this and other related experience.
