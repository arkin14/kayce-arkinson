Who I Am

Welcome to my corner of the internet! My name is Kayce Arkinson, and I'm a second year student at UNC-Chapel Hill. When I'm not training for a race, you will most likely find my nose in a book or a SCUBA mask. Growing up in coastal North Carolina, I developed a reverence for the outdoors and maritime activities in particular.

Staying active in both mind and body has been a steadfast value in my life from a young age. Many of my career and extracurricular interests stem from this foundational component of my character. I invite you to continue scrolling to learn more about my work.

What I do

Over the last year and a half of college, I've learned as much inside the classroom as I have from my experience in the workforce. From trail work in Colorado to finding small business solutions back in North Carolina, I've explored an array of unique positions.

As a result, my passions have evolved to reflect the lessons imparted by each of these opportunities. The most impactful position I've held has been working in low-income elementary schools as a nutrition educator. Running classroom programs over 4-6 week long stints, our goal is to provide young learners with exposure to healthy eating practices. Moving forward, I aim to establish a career in law focused on food policy, agriculture, and multi-national supply chain dynamics.
